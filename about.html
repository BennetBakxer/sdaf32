Our story

Why did we start this foundation?

Humanity Relief Wave was born out of a deep sense of urgency and compassion. In 2010, our founder Sophie Moore witnessed firsthand the devastating effects of the conflict in Gaza during a humanitarian mission. The overwhelming need for basic necessities such as food, shelter, and medical care inspired Sophie to take action. With a small team of dedicated volunteers, she started delivering aid to the most affected areas. What began as a small effort quickly grew into a full-fledged organization, committed to providing comprehensive support to vulnerable communities around the world.

Our mission

The mission we are working towards

Our mission is to eradicate hunger, poverty, and war by providing immediate relief and sustainable solutions to communities in crisis. We believe in a world where every person has access to basic necessities and opportunities for a better future.
