How we help?

What are we doing to assist these communities?

House & shelter

We provide safe and secure housing solutions for families who have lost their homes due to the conflict, ensuring they have a place to stay and rebuild their lives.

Love & support

We offer emotional and psychological support to help individuals cope with the trauma of war, providing counseling and mental health services.

Food & groceries

We ensure that families have access to nutritious food during these challenging times by distributing food packages and running community kitchens.

Environmental help

We work to mitigate the environmental impact of the conflict, ensuring that communities have access to clean water and safe living conditions.

Education

We provide educational resources and support to ensure that children can continue their studies despite the disruptions caused by the conflict.

Health & insurance

We offer medical aid and health insurance to cover the treatment of injuries and illnesses resulting from the conflict, ensuring everyone receives the care they need.
